Introduction

What is Customer Churn?

Churn refers to when customers stop doing business with a company or service. Predicting churn helps businesses retain valuable customers and reduce revenue loss.

  • Critical for subscription-based business models
  • Acquiring new customers costs 5-25x more than retaining existing ones
  • Early prediction enables proactive retention strategies
📊

Business Impact

Reducing churn by 5% can increase profits by 25-95%

Problem Statement

⚠️

High Churn Rates

Industries like telecom experience 15-25% annual churn, significantly impacting revenue

🔍

Black Box Models

Traditional ML models lack interpretability, making business decisions difficult

⏱️

Reactive Approach

Companies often act after customers leave, missing prevention opportunities

Objective

Develop an accurate prediction system with transparent explanations and an interactive dashboard for real-time decision making

Explainable AI (SHAP)

Why SHAP?

  • SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) provides consistent, locally accurate feature attributions
  • Explains individual predictions at the customer level
  • Identifies global feature importance across the dataset
  • Enables business stakeholders to trust and act on model outputs
